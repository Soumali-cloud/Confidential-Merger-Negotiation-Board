What Happens Next?

  1. Commitment Creation: Your financial data is bound with a cryptographic commitment hash.
  2. Proof Generation: System generates 5 zero-knowledge proofs for each condition.
  3. Digital Signature: Proofs are certified with ECDSA digital signature.
  4. Validation Check: All conditions are verified in real-time.
  5. Blockchain Submit: Proof is recorded on Midnight Network for immutable audit trail.
  6. History Recording: Proof is saved to your history with timestamp and status.
